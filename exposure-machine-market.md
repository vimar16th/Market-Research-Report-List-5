<p><p>An Exposure Machine is a device used in the manufacturing of printed circuit boards (PCBs) and semiconductor devices. It is used to transfer a pattern from a photomask onto a substrate coated with a light-sensitive material, typically a photoresist. This process is a critical step in the fabrication of PCBs and semiconductors as it defines the patterns that will ultimately determine the functionality of the final product.</p><p>The Exposure Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period.
