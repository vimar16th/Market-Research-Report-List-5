<p><ul><li>Automotive Regenerative Braking System</li><li>Automotive Waste Heat Recovery System</li><li>Kinetic Energy Recovery System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Energy Recovery Systems comprise of three main types: Automotive Regenerative Braking System, Automotive Waste Heat Recovery System, and Kinetic Energy Recovery System. Automotive Regenerative Braking System captures and stores the energy produced during braking, which can then be used to power the vehicle. Automotive Waste Heat Recovery System utilizes the wasted heat produced by the vehicle's engine to generate electricity. Kinetic Energy Recovery System converts the vehicle's kinetic energy into electricity during deceleration, which can be used to enhance fuel efficiency.</p></p>
