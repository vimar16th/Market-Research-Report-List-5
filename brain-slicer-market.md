<p><p>The Brain Slicer market is segmented into two types based on the type of suture being targeted - Coronal Suture and Sagittal Suture. The Coronal Suture market focuses on devices specifically designed for cutting through the coronal suture, which is the transverse suture located between the frontal and parietal bones of the skull. On the other hand, the Sagittal Suture market targets devices that are used to cut through the sagittal suture, which is the suture located along the top of the skull from front to back.</p></p>
