<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Reduction</li><li>Back Fed Active Noise Reduction</li><li>Hybrid Active Noise Reduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Noise Cancellation Technology Market consists of three main types: Feedforward Active Noise Reduction, Back Fed Active Noise Reduction, and Hybrid Active Noise Reduction. Feedforward ANC uses external microphones to capture incoming noise, while Back Fed ANC uses internal microphones to pick up noise feedback. Hybrid ANC combines both methods to provide more effective noise reduction. Each type has its advantages and limitations, but all aim to enhance sound quality by reducing unwanted noise interference in various audio devices.</p></p>
