<p><p>Seamless copper tubes are available in different types catering to various applications. Type K tubes have the thickest walls, making them suitable for underground and high-pressure applications. Type L tubes are commonly used for residential plumbing due to their medium thickness. Type M tubes, with the thinnest walls, are ideal for low-pressure applications. DWV (Drain, Waste, Vent) copper tubes are specifically designed for drainage systems. ACR (Air Conditioning and Refrigeration) copper tubes are used in HVAC systems. Other types of seamless copper tubes cater to specific industrial uses.</p></p>
