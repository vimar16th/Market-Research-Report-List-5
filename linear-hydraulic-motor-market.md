<p><p>Linear hydraulic motors are classified into three main types based on their design and operation: Axial Plunger Motors, Radial Piston Motors, and Other market types. Axial Plunger Motors consist of pistons that move linearly along an axial path, converting hydraulic pressure into linear motion. Radial Piston Motors have pistons arranged radially around a central shaft, providing high torque output. Other market types include specialized designs for specific applications, such as bent-axis motors or diaphragm motors, offering unique performance characteristics for various industrial needs.</p></p>
