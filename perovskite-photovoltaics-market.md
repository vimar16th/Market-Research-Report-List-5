<p><p>Perovskite photovoltaics can be categorized into two main market types based on their structure: normal structure and inverted structure. Normal structure perovskite cells have the electron transport layer located on top of the perovskite layer, while inverted structure cells have the electron transport layer below the perovskite layer. The normal structure is more commonly used and has been extensively researched, while the inverted structure is a newer development that shows promise for improved efficiency and stability in perovskite photovoltaic technology.</p></p>
