<p><p>Compact Fuel Cell market consists of various types of fuel cells including PEMFCs (Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells), SOFCs (Solid Oxide Fuel Cells), MCFCs (Molten Carbonate Fuel Cells), and DMFCs (Direct Methanol Fuel Cells). PEMFCs are widely used in vehicles and portable electronics due to their high power density and fast startup time. SOFCs are often used in stationary power generation applications. MCFCs operate at high temperatures and are suitable for industrial applications. DMFCs use methanol as fuel and are commonly used in portable electronic devices.</p></p>
