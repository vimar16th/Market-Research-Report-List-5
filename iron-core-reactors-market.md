<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Oil Immersed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron core reactors are used in electrical power systems to control voltage levels and reduce power system disturbances. Two common types are dry type and oil immersed type. Dry type iron core reactors use air as the cooling medium and are suitable for indoor applications where fire safety is a concern. On the other hand, oil immersed type iron core reactors use mineral oil as the cooling medium and are used in outdoor applications where higher power ratings are required. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining system stability and efficiency.</p></p>
