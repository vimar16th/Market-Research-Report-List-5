<p><ul><li>Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Vacuum Die Casting</li><li>Squeeze Die Casting</li><li>Semi-Solid Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Part Die Casting market includes several types: Pressure Die Casting, Vacuum Die Casting, Squeeze Die Casting, and Semi-Solid Die Casting. Pressure Die Casting involves injecting molten metal into a steel mold under high pressure. Vacuum Die Casting uses a vacuum to remove air from the mold before pouring in the molten metal. Squeeze Die Casting compresses the molten metal into the mold cavity with high pressure. Semi-Solid Die Casting involves using partially solidified metal to reduce porosity and enhance mechanical properties of the parts.</p></p>
