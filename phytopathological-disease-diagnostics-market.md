<p><p>Phytopathological Disease Diagnostics Market includes serological tests and molecular tests. Serological tests detect antibodies in plant tissues, while molecular tests detect specific DNA sequences of pathogens. Serological tests are cost-effective and easy to perform, making them suitable for routine testing. Molecular tests, on the other hand, offer high sensitivity and specificity for accurate disease diagnosis. Both types of tests play a crucial role in disease management and control strategies, helping farmers to identify and combat plant diseases effectively.</p></p>
