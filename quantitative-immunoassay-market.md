<p><p>Quantitative immunoassay market includes various types of assays such as Electrochemiluminescence Immunoassays and Bead-based Immunoassays. Electrochemiluminescence immunoassays use an electrode to generate light from a chemical reaction to measure analyte concentration. On the other hand, Bead-based immunoassays utilize beads coated with specific antibodies to capture analytes in a sample for detection. These different types of immunoassays offer researchers and healthcare professionals a range of options for quantitative analysis of proteins and other biomolecules in biological samples.</p></p>
