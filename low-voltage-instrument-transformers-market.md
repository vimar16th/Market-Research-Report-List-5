There are two main types in the market: LV Current Instrument Transformers and LV Voltage Instrument Transformers. LV Current Instrument Transformers are used to step down current levels for accurate measurement, while LV Voltage Instrument Transformers are used to step down voltage levels for safe monitoring and control of electrical systems. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and safety of low voltage electrical systems.</p></p>
