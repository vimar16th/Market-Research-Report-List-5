<p><strong>The Industrial White Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffin-based</li><li>Cycloalkyl</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial white oil is divided into two main types based on their composition: paraffin-based white oil and cycloalkyl white oil. Paraffin-based white oil is derived from straight-chain hydrocarbons, making it a versatile and affordable option for various industrial applications. On the other hand, cycloalkyl white oil is derived from cyclic hydrocarbons, offering enhanced stability and viscosity characteristics. Both types of industrial white oil are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, plastics, and food processing for their lubricating, emollient, and solubilizing properties.</p></p>
