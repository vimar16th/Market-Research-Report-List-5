<p><p>Electronic stethoscopes come in two main types: Single-Head and Dual-Head stethoscopes. Single-Head stethoscopes have one chest piece for listening to patient sounds, making them more lightweight and portable. Dual-Head stethoscopes, on the other hand, have two chest pieces allowing for better sound quality and the ability to switch between high and low frequencies. Both types offer the convenience of amplifying sound and digitally recording patient data for efficient diagnostics and monitoring.</p></p>
