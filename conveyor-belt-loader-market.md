<p><p>Conveyor Belt Loader market includes two main types: Self-Propelled and Towable Models. Self-Propelled loaders are equipped with their own engines and can move independently on the airport apron, allowing for efficient loading and unloading of baggage and cargo. Towable models, on the other hand, need to be attached to a towing vehicle in order to be moved around the airport. Both types play a crucial role in streamlining the baggage handling process in airports and improving overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
