<p><p>Lightning rod tower markets can be classified into several types including GH (ground hinged), GFL (ground fixed lattice), GJT (guyed junction tower), GFW (freestanding wire frame), and others. Each type of lightning rod tower has its own unique design and application. GH towers are hinged to the ground for easy maintenance, GFL towers are fixed lattice structures, GJT towers are supported by guy wires at junction points, and GFW towers feature a freestanding wire frame design. Other types of lightning rod towers may have different characteristics and functionalities.</p></p>
