<p><p>Germicidal Ultraviolet Lamps are categorized into two types based on their starting mechanisms: Instant Start Germicidal Ultraviolet Lamps and Preheat Germicidal Ultraviolet Lamps. Instant Start lamps do not require any warm-up time and can be turned on immediately, making them suitable for applications where quick disinfection is needed.
