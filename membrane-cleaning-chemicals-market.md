<p><strong>The Membrane Cleaning Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Caustic</li><li>Oxidants or Disinfectants</li><li>Acids</li><li>Surfactants</li><li>Chelating Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane cleaning chemicals are categorized into different types based on their composition and function. Caustic chemicals dissolve organic and inorganic deposits on membranes. Oxidants or disinfectants destroy microorganisms and pathogens. Acids are used to remove mineral scale and metal deposits. Surfactants lower the surface tension of water, making it easier to remove contaminants. Chelating agents bind with metal ions to prevent them from depositing on membranes. Each type of cleaning chemical is specifically designed to target certain types of fouling on membranes.</p></p>
