<p><p>Zirconia powders market is divided into two types based on the manufacturing methods- Hydrothermal Method Zirconia Powders and Precipitation Method Zirconia Powders. Hydrothermal method involves the use of high pressure and temperature conditions to synthesize zirconia powders with uniform particle size and high purity. On the other hand, precipitation method produces zirconia powders by chemical reactions in solution, resulting in powders with different properties than those synthesized with the hydrothermal method. Both methods cater to different industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
