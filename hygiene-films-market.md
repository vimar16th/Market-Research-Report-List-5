<p><p>Hygiene films are commonly used in various industries such as healthcare, food packaging, and personal care products. There are two main types of hygiene films: Micro-Porous Hygiene Films and Non-Porous Hygiene Films. </p><p>Micro-Porous Hygiene Films have tiny pores that allow for breathability and moisture vapor transmission, making them suitable for applications that require air and moisture control. </p><p>Non-Porous Hygiene Films, on the other hand, do not have pores and provide a barrier against fluids, making them ideal for applications where a waterproof or fluid-resistant barrier is required.</p></p>
