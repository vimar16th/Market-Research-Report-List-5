<p><p>Disposable insulin pumps market is segmented into Closed-Loop and Open-Loop systems. Closed-Loop pumps, also known as automated insulin delivery systems, continuously monitor blood glucose levels and deliver insulin as needed, providing more precise control. Open-Loop pumps require manual input of insulin doses based on blood glucose readings. Closed-Loop systems are gaining popularity due to their ability to mimic the function of a healthy pancreas, resulting in better glucose control and reduced risk of hypoglycemia.</p></p>
