<p><p>The light commercial vehicle brake caliper market includes two main types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers have one piston that applies pressure to the brake pads, while multi-piston calipers have multiple pistons for a more even distribution of pressure and improved braking performance. Single piston calipers are typically used in simpler brake systems, while multi-piston calipers are found in more advanced braking systems for increased stopping power and control.</p></p>
