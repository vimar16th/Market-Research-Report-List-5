<p><p>The valve prosthesis market comprises three main types: Transcatheter Valve, Tissue Valve, and Mechanical Valve. Transcatheter valves are inserted through a catheter and are minimally invasive. Tissue valves are made from  animal or human tissue and are biocompatible. Mechanical valves are made of durable materials like metal and require lifelong blood thinner medication. Each type of valve prosthesis offers different benefits and risks, catering to a variety of patient needs and preferences in the treatment of heart valve disorders.</p></p>
