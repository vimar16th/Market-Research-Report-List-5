<p><p>Contact lenses for presbyopia come in two main types - bifocal contact lenses and multifocal contact lenses. Bifocal contact lenses have two distinct powers within the same lens, allowing for clear vision at near and far distances. Multifocal contact lenses have multiple powers across the lens surface, providing a more gradual transition between near, intermediate, and far vision. Both types of contact lenses cater to the needs of individuals with presbyopia, offering clear vision at all distances without the need for traditional bifocal or progressive glasses.</p></p>
