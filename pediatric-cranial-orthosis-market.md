<p><p>Pediatric Cranial Orthosis market includes two main types: Active Helmets and Passive Helmets. Active Helmets are custom-made devices that actively correct head shape by applying pressure to specific areas overtime. They are often preferred for moderate to severe cases of head deformity. Passive Helmets are pre-fabricated helmets that provide gentle, constant pressure to aid in reshaping the head. They are commonly used for milder cases of head deformity. Both types of helmets aim to safely and effectively treat cranial deformities in infants and young children.</p></p>
