<p><strong>The IR Spectrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MIR</li><li>FIR</li><li>NIR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IR spectrometers are widely used in various industries for analyzing materials. There are different types of IR spectrometers based on the wavelength range they cover. MIR (mid-infrared) spectrometers operate in the range of 4000-400 cm-1 and are used for identifying functional groups in organic compounds. FIR (far-infrared) spectrometers cover wavelengths below 400 cm-1 and are utilized for studying lattice vibrations in solids. NIR (near-infrared) spectrometers operate in the range of 14000-4000 cm-1 and are commonly used for analyzing organic compounds, food, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
