<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Type</li><li>Pneumatic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive damper market types include Hydraulic Type and Pneumatic Type. Hydraulic Type dampers utilize fluid to absorb and dissipate energy, providing smooth and continuous damping forces. These dampers are commonly used in automotive suspension systems. On the other hand, Pneumatic Type dampers use air pressure to provide resistance against motion, offering adjustable damping capabilities. They are often found in applications requiring precise control over damping rates, such as in industrial machinery and aerospace systems. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining stability and control in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
