<p><p>Automatic pool cleaning machines come in three main types: Suction-side pool cleaners, which use the existing pool pump to suck up debris; Pressure-side pool cleaners, which use water pressure to move around the pool and collect dirt and debris; and Electric pool cleaners, which are powered by electricity and operate independently of the pool's filtration system. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different pool sizes, shapes, and cleaning requirements.</p></p>
