<p><ul><li>Subtractive Dichroic Filters</li><li>Additive Dichroic Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dichroic color filters can be classified into two main types: Subtractive Dichroic Filters and Additive Dichroic Filters. Subtractive Dichroic Filters work by absorbing certain wavelengths of light, resulting in the transmission of specific colors. On the other hand, Additive Dichroic Filters work by reflecting certain wavelengths of light, adding specific colors to the transmitted light. Both types of filters are used in various applications such as photography, lighting, and display technologies to manipulate and enhance color quality and vibrancy.</p></p>
