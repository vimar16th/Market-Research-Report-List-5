<p><p>Industrial Girth Gear Market includes two main types: Spur Girth Gear and Helical Girth Gear. Spur Girth Gear is characterized by straight teeth cut on the gear surface, providing high efficiency and easy maintenance. On the other hand, Helical Girth Gear is designed with angled teeth, offering smoother operation, quieter performance, and higher load capacity. Both types are widely used in various industrial applications such as mining, cement, and power generation for transmitting rotary motion between parallel shafts.</p></p>
