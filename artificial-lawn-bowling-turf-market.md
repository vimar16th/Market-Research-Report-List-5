<p><p>Artificial Lawn Bowling Turf comes in three main types - Sand-Filled, Woven, and Needlepunched. Sand-Filled turf is filled with sand to provide stability and durability, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. Woven turf is made by weaving yarn fibers together, creating a strong and resilient surface. Needlepunched turf is created by punching yarn fibers through a backing material, resulting in a durable and low-maintenance option. Each type offers unique features and benefits catering to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
