<p><p>The Jet Fuel Additives market is divided into various types based on their functions and uses. Icing inhibitors prevent the formation of ice crystals in fuel at low temperatures. Antioxidants prevent fuel oxidation and degradation. Antiknock additives improve fuel combustion efficiency. Metal deactivators prevent metal corrosion in fuel systems. Conductivity improvers enhance fuel conductivity. Corrosion inhibitors protect fuel system components from corrosion. Each type of additive plays a specific role in ensuring the safe and efficient use of jet fuel in aviation.</p></p>
