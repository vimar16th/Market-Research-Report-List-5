<p><strong>The Copper-based Catalysts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder</li><li>Lumpy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper-based catalysts are available in two main forms: powder and lumpy. Powder catalysts are finely ground and have a high surface area, making them ideal for use in heterogeneous catalysis. They are commonly used in processes such as hydrogenation and oxidation reactions. On the other hand, lumpy catalysts are larger in size and often used in fixed-bed reactors for continuous flow processes. They offer better mechanical strength and are preferred for certain industrial applications. Both types have their own advantages and are utilized based on specific requirements.</p></p>
