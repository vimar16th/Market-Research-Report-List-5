<p><strong>The Fiber Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optics</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optics market consists of two main types: Multi-Mode Fiber Optics and Single-Mode Fiber Optics. Multi-Mode Fiber Optics is designed to carry multiple light rays at the same time, making it suitable for short distances. On the other hand, Single-Mode Fiber Optics is designed to carry a single light ray, enabling it to transmit data over longer distances with higher speed and clarity. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as telecommunications, healthcare, and IT, offering reliable and efficient data transmission solutions.</p></p>
