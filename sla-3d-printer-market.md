<p><p>SLA 3D printers come in three main market types based on resolution levels: Normal resolution, High resolution, and Micro resolution. Normal resolution printers are ideal for basic prototypes and models with visible layer lines. High resolution printers produce smoother and more detailed prints suitable for intricate designs and functional parts. Micro resolution printers offer the highest level of detail and precision, making them suitable for intricate jewelry, dental molds, and other applications requiring exceptionally fine features. Each market type caters to different needs and specifications within the SLA printing industry.</p></p>
