<p><p>Foliar fertilizers are classified into four main types based on their nutrient composition: Nitrogenous fertilizers, which provide nitrogen; Phosphatic fertilizers, which contain phosphorus; Potassic fertilizers, which supply potassium; and Micronutrient fertilizers, which provide essential trace elements like iron, zinc, and manganese. Each type of fertilizer serves a specific purpose in promoting plant growth and health. Farmers and gardeners use these different types of foliar fertilizers to ensure their crops receive the necessary nutrients for optimal development and yield.</p></p>
