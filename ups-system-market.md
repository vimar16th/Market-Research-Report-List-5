<p><p>The UPS system market includes two main types: DC power supply and AC power supply. DC power supply systems provide reliable backup power for critical applications by converting AC power to DC power for uninterrupted operation during outages. AC power supply systems, on the other hand, provide backup power by converting DC power from batteries to AC power for seamless operation during power disruptions. Both types of UPS systems are essential for ensuring uninterrupted power supply in various industries and applications.</p></p>
