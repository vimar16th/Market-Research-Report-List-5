<p><p>Industrial chillers market is segmented into three main types: Air Chillers, Water Chillers, and Evaporative Condensed Chillers. Air chillers use air to cool the process fluid, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Water chillers use water as the cooling medium and are ideal for indoor applications. Evaporative condensed chillers use a combination of air and water to cool the process fluid and are energy-efficient. Each type offers different benefits and is chosen based on specific cooling requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
