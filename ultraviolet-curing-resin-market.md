<p><p>Ultraviolet-Curing Resin Market involves two main types: Radiation Curing and Cation Curing. Radiation Curing uses UV light to quickly dry and harden the resin, making it ideal for fast production processes. On the other hand, Cation Curing involves the use of a catalyst to initiate the curing process, providing more control over the curing time and allowing for customization of the final product. Both types offer efficient and cost-effective solutions for a variety of industries.</p></p>
