<p><p>The chocolate packaging market consists of two main types: rigid packaging and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging includes materials like plastic, glass, and metal, providing sturdy protection for the chocolate products inside. On the other hand, flexible packaging uses materials like plastic films and pouches, offering more versatility and convenience. Rigid packaging is often used for premium chocolate brands that aim for a luxurious presentation, while flexible packaging is more commonly used for everyday chocolate products due to its cost-effectiveness and easy customization options.</p></p>
