<p><p>The automotive brake components market is divided into three main types: drum brakes, disc brakes, and other components. Drum brakes are a traditional type of brake system using brake shoes that press against the inner surface of a rotating drum. Disc brakes, on the other hand, use brake pads squeezing a rotating disc to slow down the vehicle. Other components in the market include brake calipers, brake master cylinders, brake lines, and brake boosters, which all play crucial roles in the overall braking system of a vehicle.</p></p>
