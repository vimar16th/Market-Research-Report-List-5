<p><p>Lubricating oil additives can be categorized into various types based on their specific functions and benefits. Viscosity index improvers help maintain consistent viscosity of the oil across a range of temperatures. Dispersants prevent the formation of sludge and varnish deposits in the engine. Detergents help clean and remove any deposits that may form on engine surfaces. Inhibitor/antiwear agents reduce friction and wear on engine parts. Antioxidants help prolong the life of the oil by preventing oxidation. Other additives may include pour point depressants, corrosion inhibitors, and friction modifiers.</p></p>
