<p><p>Floating offshore wind power market types include Spar-Buoy, Semi-submersible, and Tension Leg Platform (TLP) designs. Spar-Buoy structures consist of a long, vertical cylinder that provides stability in deep waters. Semi-submersible platforms are submerged below the surface, improving stability and reducing motion. Tension Leg Platforms use tethers or tendons to anchor the platform to the seabed, allowing for increased stability in rough conditions. Each market type has its advantages and is suited for different water depths and environmental conditions.</p></p>
