<p><p>Flight conveyors are commonly categorized into two main types: Horizontal Type and Inclined Type. Horizontal type flight conveyors are designed to move materials in a straight line, typically used for long-distance transportation of bulk materials. On the other hand, Inclined type flight conveyors are used to transport materials at an incline, allowing for elevation changes within a facility. Both types of flight conveyors play a crucial role in various industries by efficiently moving materials from one point to another.</p></p>
