<p><p>Automotive resistors come in various types, including shunt resistors, voltage limiting resistors, and others. Shunt resistors are used to measure current by diverting a portion of the current away from the main circuit. Voltage limiting resistors are used to limit or regulate the voltage in a circuit to protect components from damage. Other types of resistors used in the automotive industry include power resistors, current sense resistors, and precision resistors. Each type of resistor plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficient functioning of automotive electronics and systems.</p></p>
