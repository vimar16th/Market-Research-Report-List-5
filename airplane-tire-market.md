<p><p>Bias ply tires and radial ply tires are two main types of airplane tires in the market. Bias ply tires are constructed with nylon belts overlapping at angles, providing a sturdy and durable option with a high load-carrying capacity. Radial ply tires, on the other hand, are built with layers of rubber-coated steel belts running perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering enhanced flexibility and traction. Both types have their own advantages and are selected based on specific requirements and operating conditions of the aircraft.</p></p>
