Two main types of silver powders used in this market are flake silver powder and spherical silver powder. Flake silver powder consists of irregularly shaped particles that are ideal for applications requiring high surface area and strong adhesion. Spherical silver powder, on the other hand, consists of uniformly round particles that offer better flowability and packability, making them suitable for applications requiring precise distribution of silver particles.</p></p>
