<p><p>Brass solenoid valves can be classified into two main types based on their operating status: Normally Open and Normally Closed. Normally Open valves are designed to allow the flow of fluid when the solenoid is not powered, while Normally Closed valves are designed to block the flow in their default state and only open when the solenoid is powered. Both types cater to different applications and industries, providing flexibility and control over fluid flow in various systems.</p></p>
