Single-phase meters are typically used in residential settings, while three-phase meters are common in commercial and industrial buildings. The "others" category may include special meters for specific applications or advanced technologies that do not fit into the single or three-phase categories. Each type of meter is designed to accurately track electricity consumption and improve overall energy management.</p></p>
