FISH tests detect specific genetic abnormalities in melanoma cells, CGH tests analyze DNA copy number changes, and IHC tests detect specific proteins in the cells. These diagnostic tests aid in determining the prognosis and treatment options for melanoma cancer patients, helping clinicians make informed decisions for better patient outcomes.</p></p>
