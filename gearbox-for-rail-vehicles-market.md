<p><strong>The Gearbox for Rail Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Drive</li><li>Transversal Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gearboxes for rail vehicles can be classified into two main types: longitudinal drive and transversal drive. Longitudinal drive gearboxes are designed to transmit power from the engine to the wheels in a parallel orientation, ensuring efficient power transfer and smooth operation. On the other hand, transversal drive gearboxes are used to transfer power from the engine to the wheels in a perpendicular orientation, providing better traction and stability during turns and curves on the railway tracks. Both types play a vital role in ensuring the optimal performance of rail vehicles.</p></p>
