On the other hand, fever riser sleeves are used in high-temperature casting applications to ensure proper feeding of the casting and prevent defects. Both types of riser sleeves play a crucial role in improving casting quality and efficiency by controlling the temperature and flow of the molten metal during the casting process.</p></p>
