<p><p>The cylinder head gasket market is divided into three main types: metal gaskets, non-metal gaskets, and composite material gaskets. Metal gaskets, typically made of steel or aluminum, offer high durability and resistance to extreme temperatures. Non-metal gaskets, such as those made of rubber or cork, provide flexibility and are often used in older vehicle models. Composite material gaskets incorporate the advantages of both metal and non-metal gaskets, offering a balance of durability, flexibility, and resistance to high temperatures.</p></p>
