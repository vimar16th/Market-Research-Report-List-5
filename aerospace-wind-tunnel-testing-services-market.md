<p><p>Aerospace wind tunnel testing services are classified into two main types: vertical wind tunnel and horizontal wind tunnel markets. Vertical wind tunnels simulate free fall conditions and are commonly used for skydiving and indoor skydiving training. Horizontal wind tunnels, on the other hand, simulate steady airflow and are utilized for aerodynamic testing of aircraft and other aerospace components. Both types of wind tunnels play a crucial role in determining the performance and safety of various aerospace technologies through controlled airflow simulations.</p></p>
