<p><p>Pharmaceutical sachets come in two main types: single dose and double dose sachets. Single dose sachets contain a pre-measured amount of medication for one use, making them convenient and easy to use. Double dose sachets contain a larger amount of medication for two uses, making them suitable for treatments that require multiple doses. Both single dose and double dose sachets provide a convenient and hygienic way to package pharmaceutical products for consumers.</p></p>
