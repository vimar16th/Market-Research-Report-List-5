<p><p>Waste wrap film market includes two main types: Shrink film and Stretch film. Shrink film is a type of packaging material that shrinks tightly around products when heat is applied, providing protection and tamper resistance. Stretch film, on the other hand, is a highly stretchable plastic film that is wrapped around pallet loads to secure and protect them during transportation and storage. Both types of films are widely used in various industries for packaging and logistics purposes.</p></p>
