Primary tillage equipment is used to break and loosen compacted soil, while secondary tillage equipment further prepares the soil for planting. Weeding equipment helps control unwanted vegetation, while soil aerating equipment specifically focuses on improving soil aeration and drainage. Each type of equipment plays a crucial role in soil preparation and maintenance for optimal plant growth and yield.</p></p>
