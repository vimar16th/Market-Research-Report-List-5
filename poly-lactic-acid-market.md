<p><p>Poly lactic acid (PLA) is a biodegradable polymer that is gaining popularity in various industries due to its eco-friendly properties. There are three main types of PLA based on their stereochemistry: Poly(L-lactic)acid, Poly(D-lactic)acid, and Poly(DL-lactic)acid. Poly(L-lactic)acid is derived from L-lactic acid, Poly(D-lactic)acid from D-lactic acid, and Poly(DL-lactic)acid is a racemic mixture of both L- and D-lactic acid. Each type offers unique properties and applications, making them suitable for a wide range of biodegradable products in the market.</p></p>
