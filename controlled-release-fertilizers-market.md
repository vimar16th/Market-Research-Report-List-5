<p><p>Controlled release fertilizers are categorized into different types based on their coating materials. Polymer sulfur coated urea/sulfur coated urea, Polymer-coated urea, Polymer-coated NPK fertilizer are some of the major types. Polymer sulfur coated urea slowly releases nitrogen to plants, while polymer-coated urea releases nutrients depending on the thickness of the coating. Polymer-coated NPK fertilizers provide a controlled release of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium. Other types of controlled release fertilizers include resin-coated fertilizers and coated micronutrient fertilizers.</p></p>
