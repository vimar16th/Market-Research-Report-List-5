<p><p>Wire cutting and stripping machines are available in two main types: semi-automatic machines and automatic machines. </p><p>Semi-automatic machines require manual loading and unloading of wires, but the cutting and stripping process is automated. These machines are best suited for small-scale operations with lower production volumes. </p><p>On the other hand, automatic machines are fully automated and can handle larger production volumes efficiently without the need for manual intervention. These machines are ideal for high-volume production environments where speed and accuracy are crucial.</p></p>
