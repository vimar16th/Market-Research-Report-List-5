<p><p>Capacitive Touch Controllers market includes two main types - Single-Touch (Self-Capacitive) Controllers and Multi-Touch (Mutual Capacitive) Controllers. Single-Touch controllers operate by detecting the change in capacitance when a user's finger touches the screen, allowing for basic touch functions. On the other hand, Multi-Touch controllers are more advanced, capable of detecting multiple points of contact simultaneously, enabling gestures such as zoom, swipe, and pinch. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in the touch-enabled device market.</p></p>
