<p><strong>The Leaded Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial</li><li>Radial</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Leaded inductors can be classified into three main types based on their mounting style: axial, radial, and through hole. </p><p>Axial inductors have leads that come out from both ends in the same direction, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Radial inductors have leads that come out from opposite sides in a perpendicular manner, allowing for easy mounting on a circuit board. Through hole inductors have leads that go through holes in the circuit board, providing stability and durability. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
