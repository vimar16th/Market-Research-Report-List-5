<p><p>Micro vacuum pumps are categorized into different types based on their design and operation. Diaphragm type pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create pressure differentials for suction and discharge. Electromagnetic type pumps utilize electromagnetic forces to generate pumping action. Impeller type pumps have rotating impellers to create vacuum and discharge air. There are also other types of micro vacuum pumps available in the market, each with unique features and applications, catering to specific needs of various industries.</p></p>
