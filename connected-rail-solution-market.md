<p><ul><li>Active Train Control (PTC)</li><li>Communication/computer based train control (CBTC)</li><li>Automatic/Integrated Train Control (ATC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Connected Rail Solution market consists of three main types: Active Train Control (PTC), Communication/Computer Based Train Control (CBTC), and Automatic/Integrated Train Control (ATC). PTC utilizes GPS technology to track trains and prevent collisions. CBTC involves communication between trains and a central control system to optimize traffic flow. ATC combines automatic train operation with central control systems to enhance efficiency and safety. These market types are crucial in modernizing rail systems and improving overall performance.</p></p>
