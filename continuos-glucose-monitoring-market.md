<p><ul><li>Retrospective CGM (r-CGM)</li><li>Real-time continuous glucose monitoring (RT-CGM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) market types include Retrospective CGM (r-CGM) and Real-time Continuous Glucose Monitoring (RT-CGM). r-CGM involves the review of stored glucose data after wearing a continuous glucose monitor for a period of time, providing insights into trends and patterns. On the other hand, RT-CGM provides real-time glucose readings and alerts, offering immediate feedback to users on their glucose levels. Both types play a crucial role in managing diabetes by monitoring blood sugar levels and facilitating informed decision-making for individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
