<p><p>Low Sodium Sea Salt is available in two main market types, Wet Sea Salt and Dry Sea Salt. Wet Sea Salt is harvested directly from sea water through evaporation, retaining its moisture content and natural minerals. Dry Sea Salt, on the other hand, is harvested from salt mines or through evaporation, resulting in a drier texture. Both types of sea salt contain lower sodium levels compared to regular table salt, making them a popular choice for those looking to reduce their sodium intake without compromising on flavor.</p></p>
