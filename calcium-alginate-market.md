<p><p>Calcium alginate is a versatile product used in various industries, with two main types being high M calcium alginates and high G calcium alginates. High M calcium alginates have a higher molecular weight, making them more suitable for applications requiring thickening and gelling properties. On the other hand, high G calcium alginates have a higher guluronic acid content, making them ideal for use in wound dressing and pharmaceutical applications due to their higher gel strength and faster gelation process. Each type has specific characteristics that make them suitable for different uses in the market.</p></p>
