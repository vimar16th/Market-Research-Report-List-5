<p><p>Agricultural fogging machines are primarily used for pest control and disinfection in agricultural settings. Thermal agricultural fogging machines use heat to vaporize liquid chemicals into a fog, while cold agricultural fogging machines use a high-pressure pump to create a fine mist. Thermal fogging machines are more effective for larger areas and outdoor use, while cold fogging machines are more commonly used indoors or in smaller spaces. Both types of machines offer efficient and thorough coverage for pest control and sanitization in agricultural environments.</p></p>
