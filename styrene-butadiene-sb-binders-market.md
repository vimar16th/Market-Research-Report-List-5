There are mainly two types of SB binders in the market - Emulsion Type and Solution Type. Emulsion Type binders are water-based and form a stable emulsion when mixed with water, making them easy to handle and apply. On the other hand, Solution Type binders are solvent-based and are more suitable for applications where water-resistance is required. Both types offer different properties and advantages for different applications.</p></p>
