<p><p>Industrial rock salt is primarily extracted using three main methods - conventional mining, solar evaporation, and vacuum pan evaporation. Conventional mining involves digging salt out of underground deposits, while solar evaporation involves collecting salt from saltwater ponds through natural evaporation. Vacuum pan evaporation, on the other hand, uses vacuum technology to speed up the evaporation process. Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as cost, efficiency, and environmental impact.</p></p>
