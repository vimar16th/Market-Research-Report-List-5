<p><p>Smart stencil cleaning machines come in two main market types: One Chamber and Two Chambers. A One Chamber machine has a single cleaning chamber where the stencil is cleaned using a specific cleaning solution. On the other hand, a Two Chambers machine has two separate chambers, one for cleaning and the other for rinsing the stencil. The Two Chambers machine provides a more thorough cleaning process by allowing for a separate rinsing step, ensuring a cleaner stencil surface.</p></p>
