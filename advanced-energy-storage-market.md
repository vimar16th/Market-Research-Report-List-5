<p><p>Advanced energy storage market includes various types such as Pumped Hydro Storage, Battery Storage, Flywheel Storage, Thermal Storage, and Others. Pumped Hydro Storage involves the use of surplus energy to pump water uphill which is then released to generate electricity during peak demand. Battery Storage stores excess energy in batteries for later use. Flywheel Storage stores energy in a spinning wheel while Thermal Storage stores energy in the form of heat or cold. Other types include compressed air, supercapacitors, and hydrogen storage.</p></p>
