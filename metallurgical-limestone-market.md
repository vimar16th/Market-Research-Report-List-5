<p><p>Metallurgical limestone market types include dolomitic limestone, calcitic dolomite, and magnesian limestones. Dolomitic limestone is composed mainly of calcium and magnesium carbonates. Calcitic dolomite is primarily made up of calcium carbonate and magnesium. Magnesian limestones contain a higher percentage of magnesium carbonate compared to other types. These different types of limestone are used in various industries for their unique chemical properties and applications in processes such as steel production, soil conditioning, and water treatment.</p></p>
