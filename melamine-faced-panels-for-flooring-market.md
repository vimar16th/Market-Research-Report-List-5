<p><p>Melamine-faced Panels for Flooring come in two main types: MF-C and MF-MDF. MF-C panels have a core made of chipboard and are covered with a melamine layer, providing a cost-effective option for flooring. On the other hand, MF-MDF panels have a medium-density fiberboard core and offer higher durability and smoother finish compared to MF-C panels. Both types of panels are popular choices for flooring due to their resistance to wear and tear, as well as their versatility in design options.</p></p>
