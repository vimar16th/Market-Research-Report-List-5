<p><p>Passenger vehicle bias tire market consists of two main types: ordinary bias tires and bias belted tires. Ordinary bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of textile cords laid diagonally across the tire, providing strength and stability. Bias belted tires have an extra layer of belts positioned under the tread for added reinforcement. Ordinary bias tires are affordable and durable, while bias belted tires offer better performance and handling. Both types are popular choices for passenger vehicles depending on the buyer's preferences and budget.</p></p>
