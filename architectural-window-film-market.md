<p><p>Architectural window films are divided into four main types - Solar Control Film, Safety/Security Film, Decorative Film, and Spectrally Selective Film. Solar Control Film helps regulate heat and reduce glare, improving energy efficiency. Safety/Security Film provides protection against shattering, burglaries, and vandalism. Decorative Film offers privacy and aesthetics through various patterns and designs. Spectrally Selective Film allows natural light while blocking harmful UV rays. Each type of film serves a specific purpose in enhancing the functionality and appearance of windows in architectural settings.</p></p>
