<p><p>Liquid polysulfide polymer is available in two main types based on viscosity: medium-viscosity and high-viscosity. Medium-viscosity polymers are relatively thinner and flow more easily, making them ideal for applications where flexibility and adhesion are key, such as sealants and adhesives. On the other hand, high-viscosity polymers are thicker and provide better resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for use in coatings and potting compounds where durability and protection are essential. Both types offer unique properties and advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
