<p><p>Automotive bellows are classified into three main types based on their design and orientation: Cross-ply bellows have layers of material oriented in different directions for increased flexibility, Axial bellows have folds that expand and contract in a straight line, and Cross-axial bellows combine the characteristics of both cross-ply and axial bellows. Each type is designed to accommodate specific movement requirements within automotive systems, offering flexibility, durability, and efficient functionality. These different types cater to diverse automotive applications and industry needs.</p></p>
