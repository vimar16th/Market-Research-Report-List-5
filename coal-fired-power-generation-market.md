<p><p>Coal Fired Power Generation market includes two main types of systems: Pulverized Coal Systems and Cyclone Furnaces. Pulverized Coal Systems crush coal into fine powder and burn it in a furnace to produce steam for turbines. Cyclone Furnaces use a cyclone to combust coal and capture ash before it is released into the atmosphere. Both systems generate electricity by using coal as a fuel source, but the combustion process and ash capture methods differ between the two types.</p></p>
