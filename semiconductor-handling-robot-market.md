<p><p>Semiconductor handling robots come in two main types: vacuum robotic and atmospheric robotic. Vacuum robotic systems are designed to work in a controlled environment with low pressure and are crucial for handling delicate semiconductor materials without the risk of contamination. On the other hand, atmospheric robotic systems are used in standard environments and are more versatile in handling a variety of materials. Both types play a vital role in the semiconductor industry by automating the handling and processing of sensitive materials.</p></p>
