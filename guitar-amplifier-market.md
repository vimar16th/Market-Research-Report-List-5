<p><p>Combo amplifiers and head amplifiers are two main types of guitar amplifiers present in the market. Combo amplifiers are a single unit containing both the amplifier and the speaker in one box. They are popular among beginners and musicians who prioritize portability. On the other hand, head amplifiers are separated into two units - the amplifier head and the speaker cabinet. This option allows for more flexibility in terms of customization and power output, making them popular among professional musicians and gigging performers.</p></p>
