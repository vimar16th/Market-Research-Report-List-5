<p><p>The lithium battery material market can be divided into two main types: cathode material and anode material. Cathode materials, such as lithium cobalt oxide or lithium iron phosphate, are responsible for storing and releasing positive ions during the battery's charge and discharge cycles. On the other hand, anode materials, like graphite or lithium titanate, perform the same function but with negative ions. Both types of materials play a crucial role in the overall performance and efficiency of lithium batteries.</p></p>
