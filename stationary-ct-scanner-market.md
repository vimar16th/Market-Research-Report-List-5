<p><strong>The Stationary CT Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-slice CT</li><li>Mid-slice CT</li><li>Low-slice CT</li><li>Cone-beam CT (CBCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary CT scanners are classified based on the number of slices they can produce at one time, which includes high-slice CT, mid-slice CT, and low-slice CT. High-slice CT scanners can produce a large number of slices in a short amount of time, making them ideal for detailed imaging. Mid-slice CT scanners offer a balance between speed and imaging quality, while low-slice CT scanners produce fewer slices but are more cost-effective. Cone-beam CT (CBCT) scanners use a cone-shaped X-ray beam to produce detailed 3D images of anatomical structures.</p></p>
