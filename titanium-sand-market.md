<p><p>Titanium sand market types include rutile, ilmenite, leucoxene, and others. Rutile is known for its high titanium dioxide content and is commonly used in welding rod coatings and in the manufacturing of titanium metal. Ilmenite is the most abundant source of titanium and is used in the production of pigments and titanium metal. Leucoxene is a mixture of rutile and ilmenite and is often used in the production of ceramic glazes. Other types of titanium sand include synthetic rutile and titanium slag.</p></p>
