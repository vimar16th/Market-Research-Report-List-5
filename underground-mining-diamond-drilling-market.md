<p><p>Underground Mining Diamond Drilling involves two main types of drilling methods: Rotary Drilling and Wireline Drilling. Rotary Drilling is a method where the drill bit is rotated to create boreholes in the rock, while Wireline Drilling uses a series of rods and a wireline to advance the drill bit. Rotary Drilling is commonly used for softer formations, while Wireline Drilling is preferred for hard rock formations. Both methods are crucial in extracting minerals and resources from underground mines efficiently.</p></p>
