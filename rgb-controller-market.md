<p><p>RGB controllers are devices used to control color-changing LED lights. The market can be divided into two main types: Radio Remote Control and Infrared Remote Control. Radio remote control RGB controllers operate using radio frequency signals, allowing users to control their LED lights from a distance without needing a direct line of sight. In contrast, Infrared remote control RGB controllers rely on infrared signals and require a direct line of sight between the controller and the LED lights. Both types offer convenience and flexibility in managing lighting effects.</p></p>
