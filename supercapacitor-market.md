<p><strong>The Supercapacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double layer capacitors</li><li>Pseudocapacitors</li><li>Hybrid capacitors</li><li>Composite Hybrids</li><li>Asymmetric Hybrids</li><li>Battery-type Hybrids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Supercapacitors are categorized into various types based on their electrode materials and construction. Double layer capacitors store energy through the electrostatic separation of charges. Pseudocapacitors utilize reversible faradaic reactions at their electrodes. Hybrid capacitors combine features of both double layer and pseudocapacitors. Composite hybrids use a combination of different materials for improved performance. Asymmetric hybrids have different electrodes for enhanced energy and power density. Battery-type hybrids combine the characteristics of supercapacitors and batteries for high energy storage capacity. Each type offers unique benefits for diverse applications in the market.</p></p>
