<p><p>Surface-mounted lights are fixtures that are attached to the ceiling or wall surface, providing general or task lighting. Strip lights consist of long, narrow LED bulbs that can be customized to fit various spaces. Track lights are adjustable fixtures that can be moved along a track to direct lighting where needed. Recessed lights are installed into the ceiling for a seamless appearance. Suspended lights are fixtures that hang from the ceiling, adding a decorative element to the space while providing ambient lighting.</p></p>
