<p><strong>The Hot Water Recirculation Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Hot Water Recirculation Pumps</li><li>Active Hot Water Recirculation Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive hot water recirculation pumps use the existing hot water pipes to circulate water back to the water heater, eliminating the need for a dedicated return line. These pumps are typically less expensive and easier to install than active pumps. Active hot water recirculation pumps require a dedicated return line to circulate hot water continuously, providing instant hot water at the tap. Although they may be more costly and complex to install, active pumps are often more efficient and can provide hot water more quickly.</p></p>
