<p><ul><li>Full Color Blind Glasses</li><li>Partially Color Blind Glasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Color Blind Glasses are designed for individuals who are completely color blind, also known as achromatopsia. These glasses aim to provide wearers with the ability to see the full spectrum of colors by filtering out specific wavelengths of light. Partially Color Blind Glasses are for individuals with mild to moderate color blindness, such as deuteranomaly or protanomaly. These glasses work by enhancing certain colors to help wearers better distinguish between them. Both types of glasses offer different levels of color correction depending on the severity of the color blindness.</p></p>
