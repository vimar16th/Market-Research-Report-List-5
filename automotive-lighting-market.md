<p><p>Automotive lighting market encompasses three main types of lighting technologies - Halogen, HID, and LED. Halogen lighting is the most common and affordable option, providing a warm yellowish light. HID lighting offers a brighter and clearer white light, but at a higher cost. LED lighting is the most advanced and energy-efficient option, producing a bright, long-lasting light with minimal heat generation. Each type of lighting serves different purposes and caters to varying preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
