<p><ul><li>Dynamic Stereo Camera</li><li>Static Stereo Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Stereo Camera market consists of two main types: Dynamic Stereo Camera and Static Stereo Camera. Dynamic Stereo Cameras provide real-time depth perception by continuously adjusting the camera position and angle to capture varying distances and angles. On the other hand, Static Stereo Cameras have a fixed position and capture a fixed range of depth perception, making them suitable for specific applications where the environment is stable. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in enhancing safety and functionality in autonomous vehicles and advanced driver assistance systems.</p></p>
