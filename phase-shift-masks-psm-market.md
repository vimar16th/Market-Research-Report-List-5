<p><strong>The Phase Shift Masks PSM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halftone Mask (Attenuated PSM)</li><li>Levenson Mask (Alternating PSM)</li><li>RimPSM</li><li>Sub ResolutionPSM</li><li>Self-alignmentPSM</li><li>Chrome-lessPSM /All -transparent PSM</li><li>Complex PSM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phase Shift Masks (PSM) are used in advanced semiconductor manufacturing to improve resolution and reduce optical proximity effects. There are various types of PSM including Halftone Mask, Levenson Mask, RimPSM, Sub-Resolution PSM, Self-Alignment PSM, and Chrome-less / All-Transparent PSM. Halftone Mask uses varying transmission levels to create phase shifts, while Levenson Mask alternates between phase-shifting and non-phase-shifting areas. RimPSM focuses on improving resolution at the edges of features, while Sub-Resolution PSM enhances pattern fidelity. Self-Alignment PSM aids in aligning patterns during manufacturing, and Chrome-less / All-Transparent PSM eliminates the need for chrome layers, improving transmission efficiency and resolution. Complex PSM may combine various techniques to achieve specific lithography goals.</p></p>
