<p><p>Aircraft fasteners market can be categorized into two main types: threaded fasteners and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts and screws, have helical ridges or threads that grip into the material they are fastened to, providing a secure connection. Non-threaded fasteners, like rivets and pins, do not have threads but rely on other methods such as friction or expansion to hold components together. Both types of fasteners play critical roles in securing aircraft structures and ensuring safety in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
