<p><p>Plant Sourced Organic Fertilizer can be categorized into two main types: Dry Plant Sourced Organic Fertilizer and Liquid Plant Sourced Organic Fertilizer. Dry fertilizers are granular or powdered forms that are typically mixed into the soil before planting or top-dressed onto the soil surface. On the other hand, Liquid fertilizers are diluted in water and applied directly to the plants through foliar spraying or irrigation. Both types provide essential nutrients to plants, but their application methods and nutrient availability may vary.</p></p>
