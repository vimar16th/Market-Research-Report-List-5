<p><p>Visual processor market can be divided into two main types: independent GPU and integrated GPU. Independent GPU, or discrete graphics card, is a stand-alone unit with its own memory and processing power, offering high performance for demanding graphics tasks such as gaming or professional design work. Integrated GPU, on the other hand, is integrated directly into the CPU, offering lower performance but better power efficiency and cost-effectiveness for everyday computing needs. Both types serve different market segments based on their specific performance and cost requirements.</p></p>
