<p><p>Residential air source heat pumps come in two main types - air-to-air and air-to-water. Air-to-air heat pumps transfer heat from the outside air to inside your home to warm it up during colder months. On the other hand, air-to-water heat pumps transfer heat to a water-based system, such as underfloor heating or radiators. Both types of heat pumps are energy-efficient, environmentally friendly, and provide a sustainable heating solution for residential buildings.</p></p>
